About the Band

Boys Go to Jupiter is an indie rock band known for their energetic performances and catchy melodies. Formed in 2015, they have quickly gained a loyal fanbase and continue to tour across the world. Their music blends rock, pop, and electronic influences to create a sound that's uniquely their own.
